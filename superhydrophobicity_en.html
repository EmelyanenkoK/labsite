Staff Superhydrophobivity
A special place among the directions of the laboratory in the last decade is occupied by the study of the phenomenon of superhydrophobicity. The laboratory has developed a number of methods for creating superhydrophobic coatings on various materials. On aluminum, copper and siloxane rubber, our laboratory created SOTA coatings with parameters exceeding those of coatings created by competing world scientific groups. Polyfunctional coatings were created that are characterized by increased corrosion and abrasive resistance, anti-icing properties and bactericidal action.
